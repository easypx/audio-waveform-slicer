# 🎵 Audio data preparation for Sonauto uploads

**1. Audio Slicer**: Extract specific sections and export them concatenated or individually. Imagine picking out the most important parts (assets) from your song, e.g., verse, chorus, solo.

**2. Concat Audio**: Loads multiple audio files, can change their order, and saves the concatenated audio in various formats. With this tool you can add additional assets such as custom vocals, pure instruments like a saxophone or a drum loop. (Ogg is saved 1:1 via streaming from the media recorder and is slow. Faster wasm routine is planned.)
